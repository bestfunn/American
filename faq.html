Questions & Answers

Here are answers to some common questions about Car Racing Go.

What do I need to play?

Just a web browser (like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari) on a computer, tablet, or phone. You don't need to download anything!

How are my high scores saved?

Your best scores are saved right on your own device's browser. They are private to you!

Can I reset my scores?

Yes! If you want to start over, you can clear your browser's website data. Ask a grown-up if you need help with this part.

What controls are supported?

Use the Up and Down arrow keys on your keyboard to move your car. On a phone or tablet, you can just tap the screen!

Will it work on my device?

We've made the game to work great on lots of different devices, both new and old. It should be super smooth and fun for everyone!
