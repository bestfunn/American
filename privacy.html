Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Our Promise

Car Racing Go is a fun game you play in your browser. You don't need an account to play, and we collect as little information as possible to keep you safe.

What Info We Use

  • Game Scores: Your high scores are saved on your own device in the browser. We can't see them!
  • Contact Form: If you send us a message, we'll use your email to write back to you.

Cookies

We do not use any tracking cookies. Our game is all about fun, not tracking.

Your Choices

  • You can ask a grown-up to help you clear your browser's data to erase your high scores.
  • If you play in a private or incognito window, your scores won't be saved.

Questions?

If you have any questions about privacy, just ask us using the contact form on the home page!
